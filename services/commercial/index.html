What We Do for Businesses

Commercial Door Installation

New construction or replacement — we install sectional steel doors, rolling sheet doors, fire doors, and high-speed doors for warehouses, shops, and facilities of all sizes.

Commercial Door Repair

Broken springs, damaged panels, bent tracks, failed openers — we diagnose and fix commercial door problems fast so your operation doesn't stay down.

High-Cycle Opener Service

Commercial doors cycle far more than residential. We install and service heavy-duty operators rated for high-cycle use, including 3-phase and jackshaft systems.

Spring & Tension Systems

Commercial springs carry far more load than residential springs. We replace and properly tension commercial torsion spring systems for safe, balanced operation.

Loading Dock Equipment

Dock levelers, dock seals, bumpers, and dock doors — we service and install the complete dock package to keep your loading operations safe and efficient.

Preventive Maintenance Programs

Scheduled inspections and tune-ups catch problems before they become failures. We offer maintenance agreements tailored to your door count, cycle volume, and operational needs.

Built for Business

Commercial Doors Require Commercial Expertise

Commercial garage doors are heavier, cycle more frequently, and operate under tighter safety requirements than residential doors. A residential repair tech isn't equipped — in knowledge or parts — to properly service them.

Minimal downtime is the priority

We understand that a door out of service means lost productivity. We respond fast and carry commercial-spec parts on the truck.

OSHA & safety compliance

We install and service doors that meet commercial safety standards, including entrapment protection and emergency egress requirements.

Service agreements available

Regular maintenance visits, priority scheduling, and discounted labor rates — designed for businesses that need a reliable door service partner.

Multi-door facilities welcome

Whether you have one bay or twenty, we can assess and service your full inventory in a single scheduled visit.
